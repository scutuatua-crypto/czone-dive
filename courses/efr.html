About This Course

Emergency First Response (EFR) teaches you CPR, first aid, and emergency response skills — essential knowledge for divers and non-divers alike. This course is required before taking the Rescue Diver course, and the skills you learn here could one day save a life.

What You'll Learn

  • Primary care: CPR and rescue breathing
  • Secondary care: first aid for injuries and illness
  • AED (automated defibrillator) use
  • Spinal injury management
  • Barrier devices and safe rescue techniques
  • Emergency action planning

What's Included

  • SSI EFR digital course materials
  • All training equipment and mannequins
  • EFR certification card
  • Certification valid for 2 years
